ABOUT
Hi! I am Advait, a pre-doctoral research fellow at Microsoft Research, based in Bangalore, India. Along with my work at MSR, I also collaborate with researchers at Cornell Tech and have completed my design education at IIT Bombay.

My research interests lie at the intersection of HCI, AI, and NLP. I focus on understanding user interaction with generative AI to design AI tools that preserve and enhance critical thinking, creativity, and personal expression. I am interested in examining AI's impact on individuals, social interactions, and broader societal contexts.
I use qualitative methods, such as interviews, contextual inquiry, protocol analysis, and quantitative methods, such as surveys, pre-post tests, and computational techniques, to understand and improve human-AI interaction.
